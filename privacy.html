Privacy information

Last updated: April 27, 2026

This notice describes the privacy posture of the Fighter Jet Wars mobile app as distributed on the App Store. It is written in plain terms and is not a substitute for the privacy nutrition labels, disclosures, and system controls shown by Apple on the product page, which can change independently of this document.

A1

App Store label

Apple’s product page for Fighter Jet Wars currently shows Data Not Collected — the developer is indicated as not collecting data from this app in that store disclosure. If an update to the app or store changes that summary, the version of the label shown on the official App Store page for the build you install is the one to rely on.

A2

What stays on the device

As with many games, progress, settings, and similar data may be stored locally on the device so the app can resume where you left off. That on-device data is under the control of the device user unless the operating system or a user-enabled backup (for example, device backup) copies it according to how the platform works.

A3

Apple and the store

Installing and updating the game, licensing, and related platform features are handled by Apple’s systems and policies. Parental controls, device privacy settings, and how Apple may process data in connection with the App Store are outside this app’s code.

A4

Your choices

Where a developer does not collect information as shown on the store, there is typically no account data held by the developer to export or delete in connection with that label. You can still manage device-level and Apple ID settings in iOS, and remove the app to delete its local data subject to your backup and device rules.

A5

Changes to this text

When the game or its store listing meaningfully changes, this file may be revised. The last updated line at the top will change when the wording does.
